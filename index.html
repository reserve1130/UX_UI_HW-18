Heart of Dinner
Heart of Dinner is a non-profit organization dedicated to combating isolation, depression, and food insecurity caused by COVID-19 for the homebound and low-income Asian-American immigrant elderly throughout Chinatown communities in New York.
Food Buddy
Food Buddy is a food tracking and waste management app that gives users ownership and insights into their individual food consumption habits.
Wander
Wander is a mobile app for travelers that allows split payments with friends by sharing the itinerary and having the cheapest flights available by filling out filters based on their needs.
